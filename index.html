What is StarcAI?
StarcAI is a B2B software that helps companies communicate better with their investors and the market. Companies use StarcAI to increase market optimism and stakeholder trust in their business by changing the keywords and phrasing of financial reports and letters to stakeholders. Our advanced sentiment analysis and proprietary LLM help a company induce desired reactions in its investors, leading to an empirically verified 31% increase in investor confidence and a 22% increment in investment likelihood for StarcAI rewritten text compared to the original company-generated text.
Who is this for?
StarcAI is a B2B product for public corporations and SMEs alike to improve their investor relations and not just anticipate, but also control how the market reacts to what these companies put out.
How does this work?
We analyse your text using advanced sentiment analysis, finetuned for financial reports and letters to stakeholders. We use a proprietary LLM to rewrite your text to induce your desired reactions in your investors.
